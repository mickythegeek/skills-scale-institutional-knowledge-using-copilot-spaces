# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads oversee quality assurance strategy, coordinate test planning, manage defect triage, and ensure testing standards are met throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Oversees quality assurance strategy
- Coordinates test planning
- Manages defect triage
- Ensures testing standards

### Goals
- Deliver high-quality software with minimal defects
- Establish efficient testing processes
- Support timely releases through comprehensive quality checks

### Typical Communication
- Works with Developers to assure code quality
- Coordinates with PM and PdM on release criteria and acceptance testing
- Defect reports and test status updates

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers design user interfaces and experiences, create wireframes and mockups, and conduct user research to ensure intuitive and effective product designs.

### Responsibilities
- Designs user interfaces and experience
- Creates wireframes/mockups
- Runs user research and usability testing

### Goals
- Create user-friendly and accessible interfaces
- Improve user satisfaction and engagement
- Align design with business objectives and technical feasibility

### Typical Communication
- Collaborates with Product Manager on requirements
- Works with Developer team on implementation
- Design reviews and user feedback sessions

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts define data requirements, create dashboards and reports, monitor success metrics, and provide data-driven insights to inform decision-making.

### Responsibilities
- Defines data requirements
- Creates dashboards and reports
- Monitors success metrics
- Provides data-driven insights

### Goals
- Enable data-informed decisions
- Track and improve key performance indicators
- Support product and business strategy with analytics

### Typical Communication
- Supports PM/PdM with decision inputs
- Works with Developers to define tracking needs
- Data reports and metric reviews

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers implement CI/CD pipelines, manage deployment infrastructure, monitor uptime and performance, and automate workflows to ensure reliable and efficient delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Implements CI/CD pipelines
- Manages deployment infrastructure
- Monitors uptime/performance
- Automates workflows

### Goals
- Ensure reliable and fast deployments
- Maintain system availability and performance
- Reduce manual processes through automation

### Typical Communication
- Works with Developers on delivery automation
- Coordinates with PM for release plans
- Collaborates with QA for test environments

---

## Support Engineer

### Role Summary
Support Engineers handle post-release user-reported issues, capture customer feedback, and resolve support questions to maintain product reliability and user satisfaction.

### Responsibilities
- Handles post-release user-reported issues
- Captures customer feedback
- Works to resolve support questions

### Goals
- Provide timely and effective user support
- Identify and address product issues
- Improve product based on user feedback

### Typical Communication
- Escalates recurring themes to PdM/PM and Developers
- Interacts with QA for potential regressions
- Support tickets and feedback summaries

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts capture business requirements, map processes, track KPIs, and support stakeholder alignment to ensure projects meet business needs.

### Responsibilities
- Captures business requirements
- Maps processes
- Tracks KPIs
- Supports stakeholder alignment

### Goals
- Ensure alignment between business and technical goals
- Provide clear requirements and process documentation
- Monitor business outcomes and success metrics

### Typical Communication
- Partners with Product and Project Managers to align technical and business goals
- Interfaces with End Users
- Requirement documents and stakeholder meetings
